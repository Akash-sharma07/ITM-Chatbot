# 🎓 ITM University Admission Chatbot

An AI-powered chatbot designed to assist students with admission-related queries about ITM University Gwalior. The chatbot provides instant responses regarding courses, fees, admissions, placements, hostel facilities, scholarships, and university information through an interactive conversational interface.

## Overview

The ITM University Admission Chatbot is built using Streamlit, LangChain, LangGraph, and OpenAI. It combines predefined university data with AI-generated responses to help prospective students quickly access accurate and relevant information about ITM University.

## Features

- 🎓 Admission guidance and support
- 📚 Course information and program details
- 💰 Course fees and duration lookup
- 🏠 Hostel and campus information
- 📈 Placement-related information
- 🤖 AI-powered conversational interface
- 🔊 Text-to-Speech functionality
- ⚡ Quick-access admission queries
- 💬 Chat history management
- 🎨 User-friendly Streamlit interface

## How the Chatbot Works

1. Users enter their name to begin the conversation.
2. The chatbot accepts questions related to ITM University.
3. Course-specific queries are answered directly from the university dataset.
4. General admission and university-related questions are handled using OpenAI GPT-4o.
5. Conversation history is maintained to provide contextual responses.
6. Users can listen to chatbot responses using the integrated Text-to-Speech feature.

## Supported Queries

The chatbot can answer questions such as:

- What is the fee structure for B.Tech?
- What is the MBA admission process?
- What courses are offered at ITM University?
- What are the placement opportunities?
- What hostel facilities are available?
- How can I apply for admission?
- What scholarships are offered?

The goal of this project is to simplify the admission inquiry process by providing students with instant, accurate, and accessible information about ITM University through an intelligent AI assistant.
